About

At Bigship — a ₹100 Cr ARR logistics platform — I own everything: a 90K+ seller-facing platform spanning domestic, hyperlocal, and international shipping, and a 300-user operational ERP across 3 branches. Enterprise systems, AI pipelines, financial workflows, carrier integrations. Solo.

Before that, at Programming Pathshala: a B2B assessment platform and white-labeled LMS from zero across 15+ university campuses. A Fellowship Program that generated ₹1.5 Cr+ in its first quarter. And an ICP discovery that inverted the founding assumption and repositioned the entire product.

The problems I'm drawn to are at the edge — where a workflow is breaking because the data model is wrong, where AI is being shipped as a feature when it should be infrastructure, where a revenue model is stuck inside a process nobody has productised yet. I find those and I build through them.

I started in teaching. 200+ sessions a month, learners from 5 to 60. That's where I learned to read a room. The user instinct I use every day was built there, not in a tool.

HOW I WORK
Find what's actually wrong.
The first problem statement is rarely the real one. I look for the constraint beneath the constraint.
Redirect with evidence.
Five founder decisions redirected. Every one won with data and commercial framing, not opinion.
Ship is where it starts.
Adoption is part of the spec. A product that shipped but isn't used hasn't shipped.
Know when to stop.
I built Phil. Ran the research. Killed it. That call is harder than any build.

“Long enough in product to know that shipping is never the finish — it’s where reality begins. The real work is what survives after: reduced friction, sensible details, and earned trust.”

— Divya Nautiyal
